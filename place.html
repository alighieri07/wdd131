About Chile

Chile is a long, narrow country stretching along the western edge of South America. It is home to some of the world's most diverse landscapes, from the driest desert on Earth in the north — the Atacama — to the lush forests and glaciers of Patagonia in the south. Chile's vibrant culture, world-class wine, and warm people make it one of the most fascinating destinations in the world.
